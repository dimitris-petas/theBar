DEFINITION OF BAR

Defining the bar is "a place or a counter, where one can get food and beverage service, both alcoholic and non alcoholic." Initially known as the Bar or Tavern designation. Bar comes from the word "barrier" which means something is blocking, the wood separates the worker Bar (Bartender) with guests. Wood separation or barrier is called the "Counter", the counter has other functions that are equipped with high chairs called "Bar Stools". Bars Stools made in accordance with the wishes and tastes of a bar (Owner). In the bar guests can sit back order food and drinks they want. As a separator between the guest bartender, counter also aims to avoid the drunken guests into the bar and get drinks at the Bar.
